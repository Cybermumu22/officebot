Terminal engine
Pocket is the built-in terminal (key bar, tabs, auto-reconnect). "ttyd page" embeds ttyd's own UI — fallback if Pocket misbehaves.
Terminal port
Where ttyd listens (deck-start uses 7681). Closing a tab (tap ✕, then "end?") ENDS its session. Leaving the app or switching tabs only detaches — sessions keep running.
